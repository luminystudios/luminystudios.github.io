A few common questions

What is Pentabloom Pro?+

A one-time purchase that unlocks every colour palette, plus the freedom to mix your own. Pay once and it's yours for good, and it's enabled for Family Sharing.

I bought Pro on another device. How do I get it here?+

Open Pentabloom, head to the palette or upgrade screen, and tap Restore Purchases. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, Pro comes right back.

Does Pentabloom collect my data?+

No. Pentabloom collects no personal data, with no tracking and no analytics of any kind. Everything you make and every setting you choose stays on your device. You can read the full Privacy Policy.

How do I request a refund?+

Purchases go through Apple, so refunds do too. You can request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

What devices does it need?+

Pentabloom runs on iPhone, with iOS 18 or later.
