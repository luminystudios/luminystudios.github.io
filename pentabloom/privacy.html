Privacy Policy

Pentabloom · Last updated 14 June 2026

Luminy Studios ("we") built Pentabloom with privacy as a default. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. In short: Pentabloom does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. Pentabloom does not collect any personal information. We do not ask you to create an account, and we have no servers that receive your data. The app includes no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences and settings (such as your selected palettes) are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Removing the app deletes this data.

Purchases

Pentabloom offers a one-time in-app purchase, Pentabloom Pro. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment details. Purchase handling is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Pentabloom is safe for all ages. Because we collect no data, we collect no information from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at studiosluminy@gmail.com.
